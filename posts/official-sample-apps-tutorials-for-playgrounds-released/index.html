Apple releases sample apps tutorials for Swift Playgrounds and Xcode

Apple has dropped ten (10) sample apps tutorials for newly released Playgrounds 4 and Xcode. ‘Sample Apps Tutorials’ page lists 4 chapters containing multiple apps to showcase different techniques to users like navigating between pages, fetching content from servers, handling user input and more.

On every sample app’s page, you will find project’s summary, a button to download project files, and step by step explanation of code. To follow the tutorials you will need Swift Playgrounds 4 or later on your iPad, and Xcode 13.2 or later on your Mac.

Tap/Click here to visit the official sample apps page.

Stay tuned for more updates and tutorials. Happy Playgrounding!
