18 Dec 2021

Apple releases sample apps tutorials for Swift Playgrounds and Xcode

Apple has dropped ten (10) sample apps tutorials for newly released Playgrounds 4 and Xcode. 'Sample Apps Tutorials' page lists 4 chapters containing multiple apps to showcase different techniques to users like navigating between pages, fetching content from servers, handling user input and more.
15 Dec 2021

Swift Playgrounds 4 released for iPad and Mac

Yes, the wait is finally over now. Apple just dropped the long awaited update for Swift Playgrounds, you can download the latest version from App Store (download links at bottom). It brings the ability to develop iPhone and iPad apps right from any iPad running iPadOS 15.2 or later.
07 Dec 2021

Swift Playgrounds 4 release imminent

Finally seems like the wait is about to get over and soon you will be able to install Swift Playgrounds 4 on your iPads. Apple.com still mentions vague "available late 2021" release date, but you should expect the release within a couple of weeks.
04 Oct 2021

Swift Playgrounds 4: Things we know so far

During WWDC 2021 keynote Craig Fedrighi teased Playgrounds 4 for iPad by saying that it will bring the ability to “build apps on iPad”. Later that day we saw a short demo of the new Playgrounds during “Platforms State of the Union”, a more developer centric keynote that delves deeper into the newly announced tools and technologies.
